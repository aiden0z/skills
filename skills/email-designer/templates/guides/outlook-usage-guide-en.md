## How to Use the Generated Email Template

### Step 1: Open the EML File
Double-click the generated `.eml` file. It will automatically open in Outlook in edit/draft mode.

### Step 2: Edit Content
- Click on placeholder text (e.g., "Enter Title Here") and replace it with your actual content
- To insert images: click on the image placeholder area → Insert → Picture
- To edit tables: click directly on table cells to edit

### Step 3: Add Recipients
Enter email addresses in the "To" and "Cc" fields.

### Step 4: Send
After reviewing the content, click the "Send" button.

### FAQ
- **Not in edit mode after opening?** Make sure you open it with Outlook Desktop (Windows Classic version). Mac version and New Outlook do not support X-Unsent draft mode.
- **Images not showing?** Placeholder images need to be manually replaced with actual images in Outlook.
- **Want to change colors/layout?** Go back to the AI conversation, adjust your requirements, and generate a new template.
- **Formatting broken after sending?** Avoid making major changes to the HTML structure in Outlook. Only replace text and images.
